## 🌍 English

I just launched my personal project: **uikit.studio** 🎉

Building UI with AI usually means guessing, repeated designs, and burning tokens.

That's exactly the itch I built this to fix — instead of repeating and guessing, **a solid community of real UIs** your AI can build with.

uikit.studio is a gallery of **ready-to-run UI kits**. Pick a kit, point your AI at it, and it builds your product with the *exact* same design — no guesswork.

What each kit gives you:
- A full design system — components, pages, dashboards
- A runnable React app, working out of the box
- Light + dark, full Arabic & RTL
- Open source & free (MIT)

Faster, clearer, and far less token waste.

Best part: **the whole project is open source, and every kit is built by the community** — browse them, use them, and submit your own. Everything we build, we build together 🤝

And this is just the start — we're adding hundreds of UIs over the coming weeks. 🚀
